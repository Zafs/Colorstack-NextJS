📄 ColorStack – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 25, 2025

ColorStack ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we (don't) collect and how we handle any personal information you provide.

🧠 What We Collect

We do not collect personal information unless you choose to sign in using a third-party service (like Google or Discord). In that case, basic info such as your name, email, and profile picture may be shared with us via Clerk, our authentication provider.

We do not store passwords or sensitive personal data.

We use Vercel Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool provided by Vercel, to understand how people use the site. This tool does not use cookies or track personal information.

📁 File Uploads

When you upload images or 3D files (STL, PNG, etc.), those files are processed either directly in your browser or temporarily on our servers. We do not retain or share your files after processing is complete.

🔐 Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Clerk.dev for secure user authentication
  • Vercel Analytics for privacy-first analytics

These services may collect basic info as part of their functionality. Please refer to their privacy policies for details.

💡 Your Choices

You can use most features of ColorStack without logging in. If you choose to sign in, you may request account deletion at any time by contacting us.

🧽 Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Ever.

📬 Contact Us

Questions? Concerns? Hit us up at Contact@ColorStack.app.
